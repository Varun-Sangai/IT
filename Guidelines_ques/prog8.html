Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Computer Science

Program Overview

The B.Sc. Hons Computer Science program provides a comprehensive understanding of computer science principles and applications. Students will gain theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for a successful career in the field of computer science.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, candidates must have completed their 10+2 with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in relevant subjects.

Course Details

Duration: 3 years

Course Fee: 45,500 RS per year

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum 12th grade (10+2) pass with a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Science stream.
  • Must have studied Mathematics as one of the main subjects.

Course Curriculum

The B.Sc. Hons Computer Science program consists of a total of 6 semesters. Below are the names and credits of some of the core papers:

  • Programming Fundamentals using C++ - 4 credits
  • Data Structures and Algorithms - 5 credits
  • Programming in Java - 4 credits
  • Database Management Systems - 4 credits
  • Operating Systems - 4 credits
  • Web Development - 4 credits
  • Artificial Intelligence - 5 credits
  • Software Engineering - 4 credits
  • Computer Networks - 4 credits

Future Possibilities

Graduates of the B.Sc. Hons Computer Science program have various career opportunities, including but not limited to:

  • Software Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Engineer
